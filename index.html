Overview

CorasNow is a revolutionary new platform that puts the power of solution engineering into the hands of the business. It transforms how applications are created, configured, shared, and provisioned to provide the business with a new way to meet their needs and demands. CorasCloud has a comprehensive set of features and capabilities—including App Stores and a Visual Design Canvas—that allows users to quickly and easily move to the Cloud, build and share apps, and begin realizing immediate value.

Customers

CorasCloud is an evolutionary new platform from CorasWorks that represents more than 12 years of app development innovation and $15 million in venture capital funding. The concept and architecture of CorasCloud is based on CorasWorks’ interaction with more than 1 million users from more than 1,500 organizations across the world, including several Fortune 500 companies (Pfizer, Verizon, Honeywell, eBay, ConEd, Corning, and AMD) and every major branch of the Department of Defense. This legacy of impact, combined with emerging pressures and encouragement from these customers, has driven us to innovate, design for the future, and re-imagine app dev methodologies. The result is CorasCloud.
